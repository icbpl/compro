About Us

Indo Cipanas Bonsai (ICB Plants) is a leading horticultural company specializing in the cultivation and export of high-quality bonsai trees. Based in Indonesia, we are dedicated to preserving the beauty of bonsai art through sustainable and expert cultivation techniques.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide bonsai enthusiasts worldwide with top-tier, well-cultivated bonsai trees while promoting sustainable growing practices.

Vision: To become the most trusted and innovative bonsai supplier, bridging the gap between tradition and modern horticulture.
