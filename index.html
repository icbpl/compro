OUR EXPERTISE

Professional Services

Semi-Mature Bonsai for Sale

Premium semi-mature bonsai (70-80% developed) perfect for resale, expertly pruned, wired, and root-trained for strong nebari, refined branching, and long-term health.

Wholesale Landscaping & Bulk Plants

Supplying high-volume landscaping plants for urban projects, resorts, and commercial green spaces. Our selection includes shade trees, ornamental palms, flowering shrubs, and ground covers.

Rare Tropical Plants Supplier

Exclusive rare and exotic tropical plants, including variegated Aroids, Philodendrons, Alocasias, and Orchids, sourced from Asia's biodiversity hotspots.

Competition-Ready Bonsai

Exhibition-grade bonsai masterpieces, meticulously shaped over decades for competition and high-end collections. Featuring aged Junipers, Black Pines, and Maples.

Decorative Plant Pots Supplier

Premium bonsai and ornamental plant pots, crafted from high-fired ceramic, unglazed terracotta, and concrete, designed for durability and aesthetics.

Custom Plant Sourcing & Orders

Tailored plant procurement services for nurseries, landscapers, and collectors. We source bulk landscaping trees, rare tropical species, large palms, and specialty bonsai.
